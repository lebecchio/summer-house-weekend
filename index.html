The plan

Two slow nights, one bright weekend.

We drive out Friday, settle in, and let the weekend do the rest — swimming, eating, doing very little of importance. Home again Sunday.

Itinerary

Arrival & settling in

Head out in the afternoon, claim a room, and ease into the weekend. Dinner together once everyone's landed.

27Sat

The full summer day

Swimming, sun, and long lazy meals. Bring your trunks — the water is the whole point. Nothing scheduled, everything possible.

28Sun

Tidy up & head home

A slow morning, pack up, leave the place as we found it, and drive home in the afternoon.

Packing

What to bring — and what to leave home.

Bring this 🩳

  • Swimming clothes
  • Sunscreen & sunglasses
  • Comfy clothes for two nights
  • Good shoes for a walk
  • A drink or snack to share
  • Beach games, if you've got any
  • A speaker — yes please! 🔊

If anyone has a speaker, bring it — that's the one we'd really love. Beach games and anything else fun are very welcome too.

Leave at home 🛏️

  • Bed linen — it's already there
  • Towels — provided at the house
  • Pillows & duvets — provided
  • Anything you don't want to carry

Linen is sorted at the house, so don't pack any. Just bring yourself and your swimsuit.
